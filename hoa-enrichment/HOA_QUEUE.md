## Scoring Framework

```
HOA Travel Affinity Score (0-100):
- Median home value >$5M (20 pts)
- Marina/yacht access (15 pts)
- Private aviation proximity (10 pts)
- Known adventure/travel community culture (15 pts)
- Concierge services available (10 pts)
- Notable UHNW residents — public info only (10 pts)
- Seasonal migration patterns to adventure destinations (10 pts)
- Proximity to luxury travel operators (10 pts)
```
