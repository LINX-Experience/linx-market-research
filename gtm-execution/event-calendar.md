- **Dates:** September 23-26, 2026

### ILTM Cannes (International Luxury Travel Market)
- **Dates:** November 30 - December 3, 2026
- **Location:** Palais des Festivals, Cannes, France
- **Cost:** Exhibitor packages start at ~EUR8K-15K. Buyer attendance by invitation. Media passes available.
- **LINX Relevance:** HIGH. The flagship luxury travel B2B event. 3,000+ exhibiting companies from 100+ countries. 85,000+ pre-scheduled 1:1 appointments. Direct access to luxury travel advisors (LINX curators) and ultra-luxury operators. All attendees pre-vetted.
- **Action:** Apply as exhibitor or media. Target advisor/curator recruitment. This is where LINX's operator pipeline fills.
- **Status:** Registration opens mid-2026. Mark calendar.

### ILTM North America

## TIER 2: STRATEGIC VALUE (Industry positioning + intel)

### Fort Lauderdale International Boat Show (FLIBS)
- **Dates:** October 28 - November 1, 2026
- **Location:** Fort Lauderdale, FL
- **Cost:** General admission ~$40-55. VIP packages $500-2K. Exhibitor space varies widely.
- **LINX Relevance:** MEDIUM-HIGH. Largest in-water boat show globally. Yacht broker concentration. Good for yacht vertical operator relationships. Less UHNW networking than Monaco but strong industry access.
- **Action:** Attend as visitor. Network with charter brokers. Not an exhibitor event for LINX at this stage.
- **Status:** Purchase tickets in September.

### Adventure Travel World Summit (ATWS)
- **Dates:** September 14-17, 2026
- **Location:** Quebec City, Canada
- **Cost:** ATTA member registration ~$1,500-2,500. Non-member higher. Travel + hotel additional.
- **LINX Relevance:** MEDIUM. Adventure/expedition operators concentrated here. EYOS-tier operators attend. Good for safari, expedition, and adventure vertical operator recruitment.
- **Action:** Evaluate based on operator pipeline needs. If expedition/safari vertical needs operators, attend.
- **Status:** Early pricing may be available now.

### World Travel Market London (WTM)
- **Dates:** November 3-5, 2026
- **Location:** ExCeL London
- **Cost:** Trade visitor registration free. Exhibitor stands from GBP3K+.
- **LINX Relevance:** MEDIUM. Massive scale (50K+ attendees) but broad spectrum, not luxury-focused. Good for European market intel and media exposure. Less targeted than ILTM for LINX's ICP.
- **Action:** Register as trade visitor (free). Attend for intel, not operator recruitment.
- **Status:** Registration opens mid-2026.
