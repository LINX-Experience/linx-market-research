## What Happened

Journera shut down in February 2024 because it could not reach profitability at scale. Despite $36M raised and partnerships with United Airlines, American Airlines, Hilton, IHG, Marriott, and Hyatt (325M customers, 760M reservations), the B2B data exchange model could not monetize effectively.

**LINX lesson:** B2B data exchange without transaction revenue is a dead end. LINX's commission model ensures revenue flows from actual bookings, not data licensing.
