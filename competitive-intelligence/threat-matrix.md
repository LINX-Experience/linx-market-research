## Strategic Summary

**No competitor has built trust-verified peer referral attribution for luxury travel.** This is LINX's moat.

- Fora has funding and scale but recruits strangers as advisors, not trusted peers.
- Virtuoso has operator relationships but no technology platform and no peer model.
- Mindtrip has AI but no trust verification, no transaction revenue, no community.
- Inspirato/TEC has the right demographic (25K HNW) but no referral mechanism and is distracted by integration.
- Booking has infinite capital but plays a completely different game (OTA vs. trust network).
- Ahoy Club has yacht depth but standard broker model with 3x LINX's commission rate.

**LINX's positioning is CLEAR and DEFENSIBLE.** The risk is not feature competition -- it's distribution speed. Fora and Mindtrip are growing faster than LINX can build. The counter: LINX doesn't need to outgrow them. LINX needs to own the trust layer they can't replicate.
