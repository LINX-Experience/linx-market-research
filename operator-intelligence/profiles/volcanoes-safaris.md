## Services & Positioning

Volcanoes Safaris is the world's only operator focused exclusively on great ape tourism — mountain gorilla trekking in Rwanda and Uganda, plus chimpanzee trekking. Five lodges positioned adjacent to gorilla/chimpanzee national parks. Each lodge designed by Praveen Moman to complement its natural environment.

Strategic pivot ~6 years ago: stopped tour operations to focus exclusively on lodge ownership/management. "Made the company more manageable and more profitable with the same revenue." This is exactly the asset-proximity model LINX values — owner-operator with direct control.

Gorilla permit pricing: $1,500/person (Uganda) to $1,500/person (Rwanda). Lodge rates: $400-$1,200/person/night. Combined gorilla trekking + lodge stay: $3,000-$8,000/person for a typical 3-5 night trip.
