## Archetype: Heritage Brokerage

Edmiston is old-guard luxury yacht brokerage. Nicholas Edmiston built the brand on high-touch, white-glove service. Jamie Edmiston modernized operations but the model remains broker-centric. EUR 1.25B in sales in a single year signals massive deal flow — but it's all broker-intermediated.

## LINX Value Proposition

**Pitch:** "Edmiston closed EUR 1.25 billion in yacht sales last year. How much of that originated from a past client's recommendation to a friend? LINX tracks that referral chain and attributes it — at 5% vs. the 15-20% you currently pay co-brokers."

**Hook:** Edmiston's heritage brand means repeat clients. Those clients recommend Edmiston to their networks. Today, that recommendation is invisible. LINX makes it trackable and rewardable.
