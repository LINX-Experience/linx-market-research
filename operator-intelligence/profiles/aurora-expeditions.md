## Business Model

Small-ship expedition cruise operator. Two purpose-built Ulstein X-BOW ships (Greg Mortimer and Sylvia Earle). Focus on active, expedition-style cruising with kayaking, diving, hiking, mountaineering add-ons. Expanding from polar core into new regions for 2026 (SE Asia, Mediterranean, British Isles). 10 solo cabins per sailing with no single supplement — targeting solo traveler segment. B2C direct + B2B travel advisor partnerships.

## Archetype

**Founder-Led Explorer.** Greg Mortimer (mountaineer, explorer) founded the company from genuine expedition expertise. Small fleet, active adventure positioning, expanding cautiously. Founder DNA = trust alignment with LINX.
