## What They Do

ROAR AFRICA is a female-founded luxury African safari specialist delivering fully customizable, ultra-high-end experiences across Africa's last wild spaces. Their flagship product, "The Greatest Safari on Earth," is a 12-day multi-country private jet safari priced at $175,000 per person (2026). The company operates across Zimbabwe (Matetsi Victoria Falls), Botswana (Xigera Safari Lodge), Kenya (Angama Mara), and Rwanda (Singita Kwitonda). They also offer a Kilimanjaro climb ($31,500/person) and science-meets-safari experiences like "Journey to Awe" with UC Berkeley's Prof. Dacher Keltner.

**Key differentiator:** Ultra-premium positioning. $175K/person means each booking generates massive commission potential. Female-founded, conservation-first, science-integrated.
