## What They Do

Yachtzoo is a Monaco-headquartered superyacht brokerage offering yacht sales, charter, management, new builds, and refits. Strategically positioned near Port Hercules — the epicenter of superyacht activity. Additional offices in Fort Lauderdale, London, and Kobe. Full-service offering from charter arrangement to yacht management as turnkey ownership experience.

**Key differentiator:** Monaco HQ = direct proximity to the highest concentration of superyacht owners and charterers globally. Full lifecycle service (charter, buy, manage, refit, sell).

## Archetype

**The Monaco Broker** — Yachtzoo is the classic high-end yacht brokerage. Monaco HQ signals prestige but the business model is intermediary-based. More interesting as a Monaco GP intelligence target than as a standard LINX operator.

## Top 3 Objections & Responses

1. **"Our relationships are built on decades of trust in Monaco — we don't need a platform."**
   Response: "Your relationships are your moat. LINX doesn't replace them — it captures the referrals that flow FROM them. When a charter client tells a friend at the yacht club about their experience, LINX makes that recommendation trackable and rewarding. Your relationships get credited."

2. **"We manage the full lifecycle — charter to ownership. A referral layer doesn't fit."**
   Response: "Charter-to-ownership is exactly the funnel LINX optimizes. A charter guest who loves the experience refers a friend. That friend charters, then eventually buys. LINX tracks the referral chain across the entire lifecycle — charter, buy, refit, manage."

3. **"5% commission on a charter is fine, but we already earn 15-20% as broker."**
   Response: "The 5% isn't replacing your broker fee. It's the cost of acquiring a new client through a trusted referral channel. Your existing clients recommend yachts to friends. LINX pays them for that recommendation and delivers you a pre-qualified lead."
