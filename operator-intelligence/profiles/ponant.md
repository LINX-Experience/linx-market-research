## Archetype: Corporate Luxury Expedition

Ponant is the largest and most prestigious luxury expedition cruise operator. Owned by the Pinault family's Artemis (which also owns Christie's, Kering luxury brands). This corporate pedigree means they don't need LINX — they have distribution, they have brand, they have capital. But their expedition routes overlap perfectly with LINX's target verticals (Antarctic, Arctic, remote destinations).

---

## LINX Value Proposition

**Pitch:** "Ponant guests who complete a Le Commandant Charcot voyage become the most powerful advocates in expedition travel. Their friends trust their recommendation more than any brochure. LINX turns that advocacy into tracked, attributed referrals — at 10% vs. the 15%+ you pay travel advisors."

**Hook:** Le Commandant Charcot is a one-of-a-kind vessel. Every guest who sails it becomes an ambassador. The question is whether Ponant can capture the referral value of those ambassadors. Today they can't. LINX can.
