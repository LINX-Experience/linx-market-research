## Business Model

Hill Robinson is primarily a yacht management company, not a pure brokerage. They manage yachts on behalf of owners and provide 360-degree charter management services (financial admin, deposits, APA allocation, VAT, itinerary, crew). This is a different entry point for LINX: rather than brokering charters, Hill Robinson manages the owner side. Their charter management clients need qualified charter guests — exactly what LINX referrals provide.

Employee count grew 25% last year, signaling expansion.

## Referral Dependency

As a management company, Hill Robinson depends on brokers to bring charter clients. They are the receiving end of broker referrals. Estimated 60-80% dependency on external broker networks for charter bookings on managed yachts.

## Top 3 Objections

1. **"We're a management company, not a booking platform."**
   Response: "That's exactly the point. Your managed yachts need qualified charter guests. LINX sends pre-vetted, trust-referred guests directly to your charter calendar. Lower acquisition cost than broker commissions."

2. **"Our owners are private. They don't want to be on a platform."**
   Response: "LINX doesn't list yachts publicly. Referrals are private, trust-gated, and invitation-only. Your owners' privacy is protected. Only the charter experience is shared, not ownership details."

3. **"We already work with all the major brokers."**
   Response: "LINX doesn't replace your broker network. It adds a new channel: the charter guests themselves. When a guest loves a week on a yacht you manage, their friends hear about it. LINX tracks that referral and brings the next guest to you — at 5% instead of 15%."
