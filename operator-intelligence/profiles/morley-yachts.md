## Business Model

Morley Yachts is a boutique Monaco-based brokerage specializing in sales, charter, charter management, and new build projects. Founded by Tim Morley (former crew on M/Y LEANDER, "the best charter yacht in the world" at the time), the firm operates with an intentionally small team (~5 people) to maintain bespoke, relationship-driven service. Charter fleet ranges from 26m VIVA II (EUR 80K/week) to 99m CHRISTINA O (EUR 700K/week).

## Referral Dependency

Morley's business model is fundamentally relationship-driven. As a 5-person boutique with no marketing department, virtually all business comes through personal referrals, repeat clients, and industry relationships. Estimated >80% referral dependency.
