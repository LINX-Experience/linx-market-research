## Business Model

Owner-operator model. 6 small tented camps set in private concessions — Shinde Camp, Shinde Footsteps, Kanana Camp, Okuti, Dinaka, and King Lewanika Lodge (Zambia). Expanding to 7 with Kanana Nyana (their "most luxurious to date") opening October 2026. Classic "under canvas" tented safari with modern comforts. Private concession access = exclusive game-viewing rights.

## Archetype

**Heritage Operator.** 78-year legacy. The original safari outfitter. Small-camp intimacy. Private concessions. This is the archetype that defines what luxury safari means — and exactly the kind of operator that LINX serves best.
