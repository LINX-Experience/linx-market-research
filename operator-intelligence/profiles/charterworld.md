## What They Do

CharterWorld is a luxury yacht charter agency that claims to have built the world's first online yacht charter platform. Founded by former yacht crew members (Reia was a stewardess, Matt a first mate), the company specializes in crewed, luxury, bareboat, sail, power, and catamaran yacht charters worldwide. 3,000+ luxury yachts available through their platform. Boutique approach with personal yacht inspections by staff. Services include itinerary planning, menu planning, and activity/dining recommendations.

**Key differentiator:** Digital-first heritage in yacht charter — one of the first to bring charter online. Crew-turned-founders with operational yacht knowledge.

## Archetype

**The Digital Aggregator** — CharterWorld is fundamentally a broker/aggregator platform. They sit between yacht owners/managers and charter clients. This creates a structural conflict with LINX: LINX aims to disintermediate traditional brokers by connecting referrers directly to operators. CharterWorld IS the intermediary.

## Top 3 Objections & Responses

1. **"We ARE the referral platform — why would we use another one?"**
   Response: "CharterWorld connects travelers to yachts. LINX connects travelers to travelers. Your past clients recommending charters to their friends — that's the LINX channel. You get a new acquisition source at 5% instead of whatever you spend on SEO and content marketing."

2. **"Adding another commission layer reduces our margin."**
   Response: "LINX replaces acquisition cost, not adds to it. If a charter guest refers a friend through LINX, you pay 5% on that booking. What does your current CAC look like for a net-new charter client? If it's higher than 5%, LINX is cheaper."
