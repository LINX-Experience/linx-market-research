## Business Model

Public expedition cruise company with exclusive National Geographic licensing through 2040. Multi-ship fleet serving Antarctica, Arctic, Galapagos, Alaska, and expanding destinations. B2C direct sales + B2B travel advisor partnerships. Expanded partnership with Approach Guides to provide travel advisors with customizable sales content and personalized website platforms. Scale operation — $771M revenue dwarfs most expedition operators.
