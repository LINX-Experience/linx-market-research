## What They Do

Silversea is an ultra-luxury and expedition cruise line operating 12 ships (including 3 expedition vessels: Silver Endeavour, Silver Cloud, Silver Wind). The expedition division focuses on Antarctica, Arctic, Galapagos, and remote destinations. Just completed 2025-2026 Antarctica season with 38 voyages. 2026-2027 season expanding to 41 Antarctic voyages with fly-cruise innovation via The Cormorant hotel in Puerto Williams, Chile. National Geographic partnership enriches expedition content. Enhanced Venetian Society loyalty program launching July 2026.

**Key differentiator:** Only ultra-luxury expedition cruise line backed by a major cruise group ($16.5B parent). Combines expedition access with Silversea's butler-service luxury standard.
