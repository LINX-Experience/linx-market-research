## Portfolio & Positioning

A&K Sanctuary is the owned-property division of Abercrombie & Kent, operating luxury safari lodges and camps across 7 African countries plus Peru. Philosophy: "Luxury, naturally." Recent investment includes complete rebuilds of Baines' Lodge (Okavango Delta, Botswana) and Gorilla Forest Lodge (Bwindi, Uganda). New Kitirua Plains Lodge opening early 2026 at Amboseli (Kilimanjaro views). Amazon riverboat (Pure Amazon) launched summer 2025.

Key properties: Sanctuary Olonana (Kenya, 14 glass-sided suites), Sanctuary Makanyane (South Africa, Madikwe), Sanctuary Chief's Camp (Botswana), Sanctuary Stanley's Camp (Botswana), plus camps in Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, Namibia.
