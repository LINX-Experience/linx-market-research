## Archetype: Heritage Active Luxury

B&R invented the category of luxury active travel 60 years ago. Their clients are affluent, active, curious — exactly LINX's referrer persona. When a B&R client returns from a Tuscan biking trip and tells friends over dinner, that recommendation drives $10K-$50K in bookings. B&R has no way to track or reward that. LINX does.

The recent strategic leadership appointments signal a company in transition — new marketing leadership, new website, new collections. They're actively modernizing. This is the ideal moment for a LINX conversation.

---

## LINX Value Proposition

**Pitch:** "B&R has spent 60 years building the most loyal client base in luxury active travel. Those clients recommend B&R to friends constantly — driving bookings worth millions. Today, that word-of-mouth is invisible. LINX makes it visible, trackable, and rewarded. At 10%, not 16%."

**Hook:** B&R's brand is built on the dinner-table recommendation. "You HAVE to do this trip." LINX is the infrastructure that connects that dinner-table moment to a booking — and rewards the person who made the recommendation.
