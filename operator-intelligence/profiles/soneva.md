## Services & Positioning

Soneva is one of the world's most recognized ultra-luxury resort brands. Pioneer of barefoot luxury and sustainability in hospitality. Each resort is an all-villa property with private pools, personal butlers, and emphasis on slow living. Soneva Secret (opened 2024) is the newest Maldives property. Known for innovative sustainability programs (waste-to-wealth, coral restoration, organic gardens).

Pricing: $1,500-$10,000+/night. Average stay: 5-7 nights. Target guest: UHNW travelers seeking privacy, wellness, and environmental consciousness.
