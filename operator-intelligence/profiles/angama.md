## Psychology & Approach

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Archetype** | The Legacy Builder — industry veterans who left the biggest safari brand to create something perfect |
| **Motivation** | Building the most beautiful safari lodges in Kenya; proving that small and exquisite beats large and corporate; protecting margins to reinvest in quality; guest experience above all |
| **Top 3 objections** | 1. "We've built our advisor relationships over 40 years — we trust them more than a new platform." 2. "Angama's brand speaks for itself — our press coverage is our best marketing." 3. "We're three properties, not 50 — do we really need a tech platform?" |
| **Approach strategy** | Respect the legacy, pitch the economics. "You left &Beyond because you believed in doing things better. LINX is the same philosophy applied to distribution: trust-based, quality over volume, invite-only.
