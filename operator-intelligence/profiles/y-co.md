## Archetype: Corporate Full-Service

Y.CO operates across the full yacht lifecycle: charter, sales, management, new build. This is both strength (they touch every client interaction) and weakness (they're a competitor to brokers, not just an operator). Their managed fleet of 100+ yachts means they control access — charter clients come through Y.CO, not around them.

---

## LINX Value Proposition

**Pitch:** "Your managed fleet generates word-of-mouth from charter guests. Y.CO currently captures zero value from guest-to-guest referrals. LINX tracks and rewards those referrals at 5% — vs. the 15-20% you pay external brokers."

**Hook:** Y.CO's management model means they own the client relationship post-charter. But when that client recommends the yacht to a friend, Y.CO has no tracking, no attribution, no mechanism to reward the referral. LINX closes that loop.
