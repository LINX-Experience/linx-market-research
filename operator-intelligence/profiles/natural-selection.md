## Business Model

Owner-operator of 26 camps and lodges across 3 countries. Mix of family-owned and community joint-venture properties. Portfolio includes iconic camps (Jack's Camp, San Camp, Duke's Camp, North Island Okavango) and newer builds (Mbamba Camp opened April 2025). Uncharted Africa partnership covers Makgadikgadi properties (Jack's Camp, Jack's Private Camp, San Camp, Camp Kalahari). "Uncharted Expeditions" — mobile camping safaris in Botswana. Regenerative tourism positioning: conservation-first, community employment, habitat protection.

## Archetype

**Portfolio Conservation Operator.** 26 properties across 3 countries, founded by bush guides, conservation-first DNA. This is the new generation of safari operator — consolidating small camps under professional management while maintaining authenticity. Jack's Camp alone is a bucket-list brand.
