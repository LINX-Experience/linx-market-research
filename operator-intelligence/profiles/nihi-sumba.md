## Archetype: Founder-Led Destination Lodge

NIHI is the archetype of what LINX was built for. A single, extraordinary property on a remote island that guests discover almost exclusively through word-of-mouth. Chris Burch (billionaire fashion mogul) bought a surf camp and turned it into one of the world's most exclusive lodges. James McBride runs operations with a conservation-first philosophy. 400 Sumbanese employed — the resort IS the community.

## LINX Value Proposition

**Pitch:** "NIHI Sumba is the world's best-kept secret — and your guests are the ones keeping it. Every guest who returns home and tells a friend about NIHI is driving a booking worth $5,000-$50,000. LINX tracks those referrals, rewards the referrer, and charges you 10% instead of the 15%+ you pay travel advisors."

**Hook:** NIHI's entire business model is built on word-of-mouth from the kind of people LINX serves: UHNW travelers, fashion industry executives, wellness seekers, surfers. These people don't use travel agents. They recommend to friends. LINX is the infrastructure that makes those recommendations visible and rewarded.
