## What They Do

African Bush Camps is an award-winning collection of 17 luxury safari lodges and camps across Botswana, Zimbabwe, and Zambia. The portfolio ranges from classic tented camps to riverside lodges and remote wilderness outposts. The company was founded by Beks Ndlovu, a Zimbabwean-born guide who built the operation from a single camp to a multi-country portfolio. Philosophy: privately guided, personalized experiences with exceptional guide standards. Strong conservation and community impact focus.

**Key recent developments:**
- 2024: Opened ultra-luxury Atzaro Camp in Botswana's Okavango Delta (partnership with Atzaro Group)
- Late 2026: New property construction planned
- Thorntree River Lodge evolving into wellness-focused retreat (validates LINX 5th vertical)
- 2026 special offers: partner pays 50%, children under 15 stay free at select camps
