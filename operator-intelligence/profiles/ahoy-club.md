## Business Model

Ahoy Club is both a technology platform AND an operator. They own 15 yachts outright and aggregate 3,000+ listings — positioning as the "Airbnb for superyachts." Key tech differentiator: first yacht charter to accept credit card deposits, reducing booking time from days to minutes. Acquired Floatspace (yacht booking service) to expand tech capabilities.

They operate a charter management service promising "strategic marketing and maximum ROI" for yacht owners — competing directly with traditional brokers on the management side.

## Archetype

**Platform Disruptor.** Ahoy Club isn't a traditional broker — it's a tech-forward platform backed by billionaire family money. They compete with LINX's yacht vertical prospects (Burgess, Fraser) by undercutting the traditional model with technology.
