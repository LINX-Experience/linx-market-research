## Business Model

Nomad Tanzania was formed when four pioneer safari operators merged their camps across Tanzania's wildest regions. The company owns and operates intimate camps in three of Tanzania's remotest corners: Serengeti (Lamai, Serengeti Safari Camp — mobile, following the migration), Lake Tanganyika (Greystoke Mahale — chimpanzee tracking), Nyerere/Ruaha (Sand Rivers), Tarangire (Kuro), and Ngorongoro (Entamanu).

Philosophy: "It is not about a super luxury safari with brass taps and glass walls. It is all about the best locations, the best guides and safari lodges and camps which are perfect for their setting." This is authentic, guide-led safari, not resort-style luxury. The mobile Serengeti Safari Camp (only 6 tents, following the wildebeest migration) is a signature product.

Member of Classic Portfolio — a consortium of owner-operated African safari companies.

## Referral Dependency

Very high. Nomad operates in remote Tanzania with no mass marketing. Distribution through high-end travel advisors (Scott Dunn, Yellow Zebra, Go2Africa) and word-of-mouth. Classic Portfolio membership suggests collaborative referral network with other member operators. Estimated 75-85% referral dependency.

## Top 3 Objections

1. **"We're not a technology company. We're guides who run camps."**
   Response: "That's exactly why LINX exists. You focus on the safari. LINX handles the referral tracking, commission splits, and guest attribution. Your guests already tell friends about Greystoke Mahale — we make sure those friends book with you."

2. **"We distribute through Scott Dunn, Yellow Zebra, and established advisors."**
   Response: "LINX adds a channel your advisors can't offer: peer-to-peer referrals. When a guest at Lamai tells their friend at a dinner party, that friend Googles 'Serengeti safari' and ends up anywhere. LINX captures that referral and brings them to Nomad."

3. **"We're already part of Classic Portfolio. We share referrals with other operators."**
   Response: "Classic Portfolio is operator-to-operator. LINX is guest-to-guest. Different layer, same goal: making sure the right people find the right camps through trust, not advertising."
