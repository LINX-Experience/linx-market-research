## What They Do

Song Saa is Cambodia's first luxury private island resort, occupying two private islands (Koh Ouen and Koh Bong) connected by a footbridge over a marine reserve. Villas perch on stilts above the Gulf of Thailand, each with 26 sqm private pool, outdoor bathroom, and direct ocean access. Built with sustainable materials (natural stone, traditional thatch). Female-founded, independently owned. Established Cambodia's first marine protected area with 6 full-time conservation staff. Recognized by National Geographic as a leader in sustainable tourism.

**Key differentiator:** Only luxury private island resort in Cambodia. Conservation-first (marine protected area). Independently owned — no corporate parent. Farm-to-table/ocean-to-table dining with on-site chef's garden.
