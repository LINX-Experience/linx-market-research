## Scoring Framework

```

- Revenue band $1M-$50M (15 pts): sweet spot $5-25M=15, <$1M=5, >$100M=5
- Referral dependency >50% (20 pts): >80%=20, 50-80%=15, 30-50%=10, <30%=5
- Commission arbitrage (15 pts): pays >15% now=15, 10-15%=10, <10%=5
- Brand quality / luxury positioning (10 pts)
- Technology readiness (10 pts): has API/modern site=10, basic site=5, none=0
- Approachability (10 pts): Stein direct=10, 1-degree=7, cold=3
```
